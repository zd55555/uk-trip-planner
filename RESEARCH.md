# UK Trip Research: 6 Days, $1500 Budget, HP + Beatles

## Budget Summary

**Grand total estimate: ~£882 / ~$1,100 for 2 people, 6 days.**
Buffer of ~£120 brings it to ~£1,000 / ~$1,250. Well within $1,500.

---

## Harry Potter Activities

### Warner Bros Studio Tour (Leavesden)
- Adult: **£58.50**, Child (5-15): **£47**
- Total for 2: **~£106**
- Book at wbstudiotour.co.uk, 6-8 weeks ahead minimum
- Train: London Euston to Watford Junction, ~20 min, ~£14 return per person
- Free shuttle from Watford Junction (show booking confirmation)
- Tips: bring your own food, set souvenir budget before entering, book first or last slot
- Allow 4-5 hours

### Free HP Locations in London
- **Platform 9 3/4, King's Cross** - trolley in wall, free photo (skip the paid photo service)
- **Leadenhall Market** - Diagon Alley entrance in first film (Bull's Head Passage)
- **Borough Market** - Leaky Cauldron entrance in Prisoner of Azkaban (Stoney Street)
- **Millennium Bridge** - destroyed by Death Eaters in Half-Blood Prince
- **Australia House (exterior)** - Gringotts Bank exterior, on the Strand
- **Piccadilly Circus** - Death Eaters fly through here

## Beatles Activities

### London
- **Abbey Road crossing** - FREE. Nearest tube: St John's Wood. Live webcam at abbeyroad.com/crossing.

### Liverpool
- **The Beatles Story Museum (Albert Dock)** - Adult £14.95, Child £9.00. Total: ~£24. Allow 2-3 hours.
- **Cavern Club (Mathew Street)** - Adult £6-8.50, Child (12-17 with adult) £3. Total: ~£9. Cashless only. Live music daily.
- **Strawberry Field** - Adult £12.45 (10% off advance online), Child FREE. Total: ~£11.
- **Penny Lane** - FREE. See the bus shelter, the barber shop (still operating). 30-60 min.
- **Beatles walking tour** - GetYourGuide ~£13pp = £26 total. Or free walking tours at freetoursbyfoot.com.

## Transport

### London to Liverpool (return for 2)
- Advance train tickets: ~£15-30 each way per person
- Book 4-8 weeks ahead on thetrainline.com
- Journey: ~2h15 direct (Avanti West Coast)
- Budget for 2 returns: **~£120**

### London (Oyster/contactless)
- Daily cap Zones 1-2: £8.90/person
- 13yo gets child fare with Zip Oyster card (apply 4+ weeks ahead at tfl.gov.uk)
- Budget: **~£71** (4 days x 2 people)

### Leavesden
- Train to Watford Junction + return for 2: **~£28**
- Shuttle: FREE

### Liverpool local
- Walking covers most city centre sites
- Bus to Penny Lane/Strawberry Field: ~£5 day ticket
- Budget: **~£12**

**Total transport: ~£230**

- Dinner: Nando's, Wetherspoons, market food, £15-25
